Percent Humidity

Humidity can be found anywhere!

For the data I pulled, humidity was a bit heightened in regions that were close to the equator; however, the data did not suggest that this was a ubiquitous trend. Even some locations far in in the Northern hemisphere had humidiites in the 90-100% range. Interestingly enough, the Southern hemisphere did look, on average, to have lower humidity ranges, overall.
