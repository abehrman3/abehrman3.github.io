Wind Speed

They don't call it the Windy City for nothing...

After visualizing wind speed vs. lattitude, it would appear fairly evenly distributed with some clear outliers. Overall, cities closer to the equator tend to have fairly lower wind speeds, but it looks like Norther hemisphere cities have some strong variation in their wind speeds. Perhaps Chiacago, which sits at ~41 degrees lattitude, is one of those outliers boasting wind speeds over 30mph!
