Max Temperature

It gets hotter as you approach the equator... but wait, there's more

As you probably would suspect, the max temperature tends to increase as one approaches the equator (found at 0 degrees lattitude). However, what you might not have guessed is that the northern hemisphere tends to have cooler temperatures than the southern hemisphere, even at roughly the same overall distance from the equator! Since the sun shines towards the equator, the tilt of the planet may be the cause of this temperature difference during this time of year.
