Cloudiness

The London fog is real...

Cloudiness may be the most evenly distributed dataset that I found; howver, even here, there are some pretty interesting insights. Cities close to the equator and in the Southern Hemisphere are less cloudy than those found in the Northern Hemisphere. As one enters the 40-60 degrees lattitude range, cloudiness percentages above 80% become much more consolidated. The city of London, England sits at ~51 degrees lattitude.
